Statements

Please respond to the following essay prompts as fully as possible. Keep in mind, these essays are your opportunity to emphasize why this program is the right fit for you and vice versa. 

IMPORTANT: Each statement should be no more than 2000 characters (not including spaces). To avoid any errors with timeouts or browser complications, please consider writing your responses in a separate location and then copying and pasting the text into this section of your application.

*Statement of Purpose

Briefly explain why you would like to participate in your first-choice program. Why would you like to study this particular civilization, in this location? How do you anticipate this experience will impact your future academic path? How do you imagine this experience might help you reach your personal and/or professional goals? If you will rank alternative choices at the end of this application, we encourage you to comment on your alternate(s) in the next question.

*Alternative Location Statement

At the end of this application, you will have the opportunity to rank up to 3 alternate programs. If you choose to rank alternates, please comment briefly on your interest in your alternative choices here. Please include the names and terms of the programs you ranked in your response. 

*Additional Statement Topic

ALL APPLICANTS: Please select one of the following question prompts to answer in the subsequent essay field.

Pretend you’re a tour guide for somewhere you call home – whether a place, a group of people, an experience, etc. What would you show your audience? (inspired by Andrew Chen, AB ’23)
